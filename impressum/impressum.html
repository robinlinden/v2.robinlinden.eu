What is it?

It's a contact-form thing for static (html only) websites, so that's what I use it for.

What do they store?

They store the full contents of the last 100 emails sent via the contact form. However, all data you enter into my contact form is PGP-encrypted with a 4096-bit key (uncrackable* even by the NSA), so they can't see anything besides the fact that I got an email from someone using my website.

How do I opt out?

If you don't want your emails passing through them, just send a regular email instead of using the form.
